About Kidsverse FutureHub

Built by educators and technologists who understand real-world decisions.

Kidsverse FutureHub is run by Vinay Sharma, Founder of Kidsverse and Stone Field International School. The platform helps students, parents and businesses choose the right path before they invest time, money or effort.

Founder-led vision

Run by Vinay Sharma

Vinay Sharma brings a rare mix of large-scale technology experience, education leadership and hands-on digital product building. He has worked across major technology environments including UIDAI architecture, TCS, HCL and Camp K12, and now uses that experience to make future-ready learning and digital solutions accessible from Rehan, Himachal Pradesh to learners and customers across India and abroad.

As Founder of Kidsverse and Stone Field International School, his long-term vision is simple: help students, parents and local businesses make better decisions with the support of technology, AI and practical mentorship.

What we believe

We do not sell first. We understand first.

FutureHub was created because parents, students and business owners often need clarity before they need a course, website or service package.

For Parents

We help parents understand what their child should learn next: coding, AI, communication, confidence, projects or a guided learning path.

For Students

We guide school and college students toward skills, projects, internships, future roles and interview confidence.

For Businesses

We help business owners choose the right website, social media, lead system, AI chatbot or automation path based on their actual need.
