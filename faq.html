Friendly FAQ

Ask us anything. No question is too small.

Kidsverse FutureHub is built to be student-friendly, parent-friendly and customer-friendly. Before you choose a course, internship, website, social media plan or AI solution, you can speak with us openly and without pressure.

Our promise

Customer happiness first, always.

We listen first, understand the goal, and then suggest the right path. For students, that may be coding, AI, live projects, interviews or entrepreneurship. For parents, it may be choosing the right course. For businesses, it may be a website, social media, lead system or automation.

We do not believe in disturbing people with repeated follow-ups. If you are not interested, or if your requirement is not something we can fulfil properly, we respect that. Our work should move forward only when you feel the solution is right for you.

General Guidance

For anyone who wants to ask first and decide later.

Can I speak with you before choosing any course or service?

Yes. We prefer that. A short conversation helps us understand the learner, parent concern or business goal before suggesting a course, project, package or service plan.

What if I am not sure what I need?

That is completely fine. Book a slot or send us a WhatsApp message. We will understand the requirement and suggest the next step without pressure.

Will you force a course or package after the discussion?

No. Our approach is guidance first. We explain the right option, expected outcome and next step clearly so you can decide comfortably.

Will you keep following up if I am not interested?

No. We do not believe in unwanted follow-ups. If you are not interested, or if your requirement is not the right fit for us, we respect your decision and keep the experience stress-free.

02

For Parents

For parents choosing the right learning path for their child.

My child is a beginner. Will they feel uncomfortable?

No. Beginners are welcome. We keep the first steps friendly, practical and confidence-building. If a child needs extra comfort, we can suggest 1:1 live guidance before moving to a group format.

Do parents get updates or progress discussions?

Yes. We keep parents involved where needed, especially for younger learners. Parents can discuss progress, confidence, project outcomes and the next learning step.

Can you help us choose between 1:1 and group classes?

Yes. We suggest the class format based on the child’s confidence, age, learning goal and attention needs. Some children grow faster in 1:1 guidance, while others enjoy small-group energy.

03

For School Students

For Grade 3 onward learners exploring coding, AI and projects.

Do you only teach fixed courses?

No. We offer structured paths, but we do not restrict students to one fixed syllabus. We hand-hold learners based on their grade, interest, confidence and goal.

What can school students learn at FutureHub?

Students can learn creative coding, Python, web development, prompt engineering, AI chatbots, AI agents, projects and presentation skills. The path changes based on age and readiness.

Will students build something they can show?

Yes. We focus on visible outcomes such as games, websites, AI chatbots, mini apps, project demos and presentation confidence.

04

For College Students

For internships, live projects, interviews and startup confidence.

Do college students work on live projects during internships?

Yes. Our internship and project support focuses on practical work. Students can build portfolio-ready projects, improve GitHub, prepare for viva, strengthen resumes and practice interview conversations.

Can you help college students crack interviews?

Yes. We support resume building, project explanation, mock interview practice, coding/project confidence and communication. The aim is to help students explain their work clearly, not just complete a certificate.

Can you guide students who want to start their own entrepreneurial journey?

Yes. We can help students shape a practical idea, build a first website or app, understand digital presence, create a pitch, and learn how technology can support a small startup or service.

05

For Businesses

For owners who want websites, branding, leads, social media or AI tools.

Can a business owner ask for help even if they know nothing about technology?

Absolutely. You can ask in simple language. We can explain websites, hosting, branding, Google Business, Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp leads, AI chatbots and automation in a practical way.

Which businesses can FutureHub support?

We can support schools, colleges, coaching institutes, salons, clinics, stores, cafes, tutors, personal brands, creators, local service businesses, NGOs, startups and small shops that want a stronger digital presence.

Can you handle social media and lead generation together?

Yes. We can connect content, Facebook/Instagram handling, landing pages, WhatsApp enquiries and simple lead tracking so the business has a clearer growth system.

06

For Schools and Colleges

For institutions planning workshops, clubs, hackathons or digital systems.

Can schools and colleges invite FutureHub for workshops?

Yes. We can plan AI workshops, coding clubs, hackathons, final year project support, internship readiness programs, digital admission systems and teacher or team upskilling sessions.

Can you run a coding club or AI club for students?

Yes. We can design monthly or short-term programs with practical activities, projects, demos and certificates based on the grade or college level.

Can you help institutions with admission or enquiry systems?

Yes. We can create admission pages, enquiry forms, WhatsApp flows, FAQ pages, AI chatbots and lead tracking systems for schools, colleges and training institutes.

Still unsure?

Tell us your situation. We will guide you.

Parents, students, college learners and business owners can ask freely. Our goal is to make the next step clear and comfortable.
